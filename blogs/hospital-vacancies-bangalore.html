1. Why Bangalore is a Healthcare Job Hub

Bangalore has emerged as India's premier healthcare destination. Factors driving hospital vacancies in Bangalore include:

  • Medical Tourism Hub: Bangalore attracts patients from across India and abroad
  • Corporate Hospital Expansion: Apollo, Manipal, Fortis are expanding their Bangalore footprint
  • Startup Ecosystem: Health-tech startups creating new job roles
  • Quality of Life: Pleasant climate and cosmopolitan culture attract talent
